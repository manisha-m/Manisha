After high school, I want to go to a university that's going to help me pursue my dream of going into the medical field and becoming a pediatrician. Ever since I was little, I always dreamed of become some sort of a doctor. Whether it was a pathologist or a coroner. My parents have always wanted to see their little girl become someone who others could always look up to for their needs and worries.

Along with going to collage, it has always been a dream of mine to travel the world with 1 or 2 very close people in my life. But traveling the world comes after I'm settled down, debt free, and have a well paying job.
